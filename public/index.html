LINKEDIN

  1. Go to linkedin.com/developers → Create App
  2. Fill in app name, associate it with your LinkedIn Company Page
  3. Under Products, add Sign In with LinkedIn using OpenID Connect and Share on LinkedIn
  4. Go to Auth tab → copy Client ID and generate a Client Secret
  5. Under OAuth 2.0 settings, add redirect URL:
    https://social-brand-studio-production.up.railway.app/api/oauth/linkedin/callback
  6. Add LINKEDIN_CLIENT_ID, LINKEDIN_CLIENT_SECRET, LINKEDIN_REDIRECT_URI to your environment variables
  7. Click Connect LinkedIn above — you'll be redirected to authorize
INSTAGRAM
Not connected
Requires an Instagram Business or Creator account linked to a Facebook Page.
  1. Go to developers.facebook.com → Create App → choose Business
  2. Add the Instagram Graph API product
  3. Go to Settings → Basic → copy App ID and App Secret
  4. Under Facebook Login → Settings, add redirect URL:
    https://social-brand-studio-production.up.railway.app/api/oauth/instagram/callback
  5. Add FACEBOOK_APP_ID and FACEBOOK_APP_SECRET to your environment variables
  6. Click Connect Instagram above — you'll be redirected to authorize via Facebook

The Machine

        
The infrastructure between your content and cash

        
Most people create content and wonder why it doesn't convert. The machine fixes that. For each of your 3 content pillars, you define one contextual CTA that leads to a generous free resource — and both resources point to your 2 monetisation paths. Every post you create from here will know exactly where it's sending people.
